## 2. Overview

This website is designed with privacy in mind. We use **no cookies** for analytics, serve **no third-party tracking scripts**, and host **all resources** (fonts, scripts, analytics) on our own infrastructure.

## 3. Hosting

This website is hosted on a virtual private server (VPS) operated by **[Hosting Provider, e.g., Hetzner Online GmbH]**, located in **[Data center location, e.g., Falkenstein, Germany]**.

When you visit this website, the web server automatically logs connection data (server log files). This includes:

- IP address (anonymised after processing)
- Date and time of the request
- Page visited (URL)
- Referrer URL
- Browser type and version
- Operating system

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in ensuring the secure and stable operation of the website.

**Retention:** Server log files are deleted after 14 days.

## 4. Analytics

We use **Umami**, a self-hosted, privacy-focused analytics tool. Umami is hosted on our own server infrastructure at `analytics.inferops.dev`.

Umami:

- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** collect personal data
- Does **not** track users across websites
- Complies with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR **without** requiring a cookie consent banner

Only aggregated, anonymous usage statistics are collected (page views, referrer, device type, country). No individual user profiles are created.

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in understanding website usage to improve content.

## 5. CDN and DDoS Protection

We use **Cloudflare** (Cloudflare, Inc.) for DNS management and DDoS protection. Cloudflare may process connection metadata (IP addresses, request headers) to provide its security services.

Cloudflare may set a `__cf_bm` cookie, which is classified as **strictly necessary** for bot protection and does not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive.

For more information, see [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in protecting the website from attacks.

## 6. Contact

If you contact us by email, the data you provide (email address, name, message content) will be stored and processed for the purpose of handling your inquiry.

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

**Retention:** Correspondence is retained for the duration of the business relationship, then deleted unless legal retention periods apply.

## 7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** (Art. 15 GDPR)
- **Right to rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR)
- **Right to erasure** (Art. 17 GDPR)
- **Right to restriction of processing** (Art. 18 GDPR)
- **Right to data portability** (Art. 20 GDPR)
- **Right to object** (Art. 21 GDPR)
- **Right to withdraw consent** (Art. 7(3) GDPR)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: hello@inferops.dev

## 8. Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. The competent authority is typically the data protection authority of the German federal state in which you reside or in which the data controller is located.
